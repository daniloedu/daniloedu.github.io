Intro

Danilo Salazar is the founder and current CEO of Origo Solutions, a startup dedicated to delivering innovative cloud, mobile, AI/ML, and IoT solutions to businesses of all types. With a vision of equitable technology access, Danilo empowers organizations through his expertise and leadership.

As a member of prestigious programs such as the MIT Entrepreneurship Network, Young Leaders of the Americas (YLAI), and a Latin America and Caribbean Fellow from FIU, Danilo has honed his skills in leadership, entrepreneurship, and technology. He has also acquired recent experience in successfully implementing various projects leveraging Amazon Web Services (AWS), including Amazon AppStream 2.0, Amazon WorkSpaces, and Amazon Connect.

Also, as a recipient of prestigious scholarships from Udacity, including the AWS Machine Learning Scholarship and the Bertelsmann Technology Scholarship Program, Danilo has honed his expertise in AI and machine learning. Currently, his company is actively collaborating with the OpenAI API, further expanding their capabilities in the AI domain.

Danilo excels in leading and empowering multicultural and globally distributed teams, strategic planning, agile project management (PMP), and agile development methodologies. His expertise extends to telecommunications technologies, customer experience solutions, data engineering, enterprise systems, and cloud architectures.

Fluent in both English and Spanish, Danilo holds a master's degree in Engineering Management and brings over nine years of professional experience in the technology industry. He has successfully managed complex projects with budgets exceeding $10 million, acting as a project manager and account manager for key customers, overseeing the end-to-end sales process and conflict resolution.

Notably, Danilo's recent experience includes implementing AWS POCs for major companies in the oil services and global B2B payment sectors, enhancing cloud transformation for multinational private equity institutions, enabling B2C transformations with Amazon Connect for high-end jewelry companies, and facilitating remote learning initiatives for educational institutions.

With his strong technical acumen, exceptional leadership skills, and extensive experience, Danilo is a valuable asset in leading telecommunications, driving agile software development teams, and implementing effective cloud solutions.

Always ready to translate customer requirements into technical specifications to deliver the best solutions. Furthermore, I aim to always deliver a job well before a deadline.
